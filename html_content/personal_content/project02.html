Angry Bots - Interactive Audio Demo

Top-down shooter game (Unity | C# | Wwise), updated to showcase interactive audio

Audio Programming | Music & Sound Design

  • Set up a Wwise project tailored to gameplay needs, including music composition, sound effects, and dynamic audio integration.
  • Composed four music tracks, each corresponding to a different game section, with dual-layered music that activates based on enemy kills.
  • Designed seamless transitions, including rhythm changes in the final section, linking to the "you win" music with a matching rhythm but different melody.
  • Created and implemented enemy, weapon, and ambient SFX with multiple variations and randomized pitch for increased replayability.
  • Integrated inside and outside ambiences that switch based on player location, enhancing immersion.
  • Built custom audio components using RTPCs, switches, and state changes to control music layers, weapon impacts, footstep sounds, and stage transitions.
  • Converted the original UnityScript project to C# to update from Unity4 to Unity 2022.
