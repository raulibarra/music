Echoes of the Forgotten Isle

3D TPS Game (Unity | C# | Wwise)

Audio Programming | Music & Sound Design

  • Composed a neofolk orchestral score featuring medieval strings, tribal percussion, and synths, using real-time modal shifts (Dorian for exploration, Phrygian for combat) to create a dynamic soundtrack.
  • Engineered a biome-based acoustic system with unique ambient soundscapes for 5 distinct environments.
  • Implemented a Wwise-driven adaptive music system for seamless transitions between exploration and combat states, inspired by AAA open-world RPGs.
  • Integrated Google Resonance for spatial audio, enhancing directional cues for enemy movement and environmental effects.
  • Designed sounds for enemies (footsteps, exhorts, attacks), the main character and soundscapes (forest, sea, birds flying, etc).

I also developed the game itself, including the gameplay mechanics, level design, and overall user experience.
